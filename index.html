About Me

I am pursuing a BSc (Hons) in Network Systems Engineering at the University of Sunderland. Previously, I completed a Higher Diploma in Network Technology and Cyber Security. I’ve hands-on experience with tools like Wazuh, pfSense, MySQL, and Active Directory.

My journey began with curiosity about how systems work behind the scenes, which evolved into a passion for securing them. I enjoy exploring both offensive and defensive cybersecurity techniques.
